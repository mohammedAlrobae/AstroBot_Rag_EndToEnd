# 🚀 AstroBot — Mission Control Assistant

Welcome to **AstroBot**, your personal assistant for the NASA Artemis II mission.

## What I can do

- **Mission Details** — Crew, spacecraft systems, procedures, timelines
- **Technical Questions** — Orion, SLS, ICPS, ground systems
- **Emergency Protocols** — Step-by-step procedures for critical scenarios
- **General Space Knowledge** — Spaceflight, orbital mechanics, astronaut health

## Data Source

All mission-specific answers come from the **Artemis II Reference Guide** (138 pages), stored in a vector database for fast retrieval.
